Instruction

  • To use the scheduler, enter your schedule in the following format:
    r1(x)w2(y)r3(z)...
  • r stands for READ, w stands for WRITE, and the numbers represent the transaction IDs. The letters in parentheses represent the objects being read or written.
  • Click on the "Solve" button to obtain the response.
  • Click on the "Clear" button to clear the previous response.
